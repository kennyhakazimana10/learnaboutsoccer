Soccer Positions

Understanding the different roles on the field

Forwards

Forwards are primarily responsible for scoring goals and creating offensive opportunities. They often play closest to the opponent's goal.

Midfielders

Midfielders control the flow of the game by linking defense and attack. They help in both offensive and defensive plays.

Defenders

Defenders protect their team's goal by stopping opposing players from getting scoring opportunities. They focus on tackling and positioning.

Goalkeeper

The goalkeeper is the last line of defense, responsible for preventing the ball from entering the goal. They are the only player allowed to use their hands within the penalty area.
